Safety Guidelines for Construction and Workplace:

1. Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) is mandatory on construction sites. Workers must wear helmets, gloves, safety boots, and reflective vests.
2. Operating heavy machinery requires training and authorization. Unauthorized operation is strictly prohibited.
3. Always keep a safe distance from moving machinery and vehicles.
4. Fire extinguishers and first aid kits must be accessible at all times.
5. Report unsafe conditions immediately to supervisors.
6. Safety violations can result in accidents, injuries, or fines.
