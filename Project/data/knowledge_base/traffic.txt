Traffic Rules and Regulations:

1. Traffic lights: Red = Stop, Yellow = Prepare to stop, Green = Go.
2. Pedestrians always have the right of way at crosswalks.
3. Speed limits must be obeyed: Urban roads = 50 km/h, Highways = 100 km/h (example limits).
4. Parking regulations: No parking in red zones or near intersections.
5. Vehicles must maintain lane discipline. Overtaking should be done on designated lanes only.
6. Violating traffic rules can result in fines or penalties.
