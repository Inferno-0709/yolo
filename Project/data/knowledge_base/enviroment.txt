Environmental Protection Guidelines:

1. Dumping waste in public areas, parks, or rivers is prohibited.
2. Trees and plants in protected areas must not be damaged or removed.
3. Wildlife should not be disturbed; feeding or capturing animals is forbidden.
4. Pollution control: Emissions from vehicles and factories must comply with legal limits.
5. Report environmental hazards, illegal dumping, or deforestation to local authorities.
6. Community participation in cleanliness drives and green initiatives is encouraged.
